## 2. The Problem
Bitcoin is the world's most secure and decentralized blockchain, but building on it has historically been difficult:
*   **High Barrier to Entry:** Developing smart contracts on Bitcoin (OP_NET) requires specialized knowledge of WASM and intricate tooling.
*   **Lack of Trust Tools:** New projects often struggle to prove legitimacy due to the absence of easily verifiable liquidity locks and team vesting contracts on L1.
*   **Fragmented Ecosystem:** Developers currently have to cobble together disparate tools for deployment, fundraising, and management.
